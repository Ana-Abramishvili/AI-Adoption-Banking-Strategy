# KPI Framework – Measuring AI Adoption Impact in Banking

## Overview

This document defines key performance indicators (KPIs) used to measure the success of Generative AI adoption in banking processes.

The focus is on evaluating **efficiency, customer experience, cost reduction, and adoption rate** across different AI use cases.

---

## 1. Customer Support AI Assistant KPIs

### Primary KPIs

- **Average Response Time**
  - Measures how quickly customer inquiries are resolved
  - Target: Significant reduction compared to human-only support

- **Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)**
  - Measures user satisfaction after interaction
  - Target: Improvement in customer experience

- **First Contact Resolution Rate**
  - Percentage of issues resolved without escalation
  - Target: Increase over baseline

### Operational KPIs

- Number of tickets handled by AI
- Escalation rate to human agents
- Cost per customer interaction

---

## 2. Internal Knowledge AI Assistant KPIs

### Primary KPIs

- **Time to Information Access**
  - Measures how long employees take to find needed information
  - Target: Reduction in search time

- **Employee Productivity Improvement**
  - Measured via time saved per task
  - Target: Increase efficiency across departments

- **Onboarding Time Reduction**
  - Time required for new employees to become productive
  - Target: Faster onboarding process

### Adoption KPIs

- Number of active users
- Frequency of AI assistant usage
- Department-level adoption rate

---

## 3. AI-Powered Marketing Assistant KPIs

### Business KPIs

- **Conversion Rate**
  - Percentage of users who complete desired actions
  - Target: Increase through better targeting

- **Campaign ROI**
  - Return on investment from marketing campaigns
  - Target: Higher profitability per campaign

- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
  - Cost of acquiring a new customer
  - Target: Reduction through optimization

### Analytical KPIs

- Accuracy of customer segmentation
- Campaign performance prediction accuracy
- Time required for campaign setup and analysis

---

## 4. AI Adoption & Governance KPIs (Cross-functional)

These KPIs measure overall AI adoption maturity in the organization:

- **AI Adoption Rate**
  - Percentage of teams actively using AI tools

- **Process Automation Rate**
  - Share of tasks partially or fully automated using AI

- **User Engagement with AI Tools**
  - Frequency and depth of AI usage across departments

- **AI Issue Rate**
  - Number of incorrect or unsafe AI outputs detected

---

## 5. Measurement Approach

To ensure accurate tracking of KPIs:

- Use dashboards (Power BI / BI tools) for real-time monitoring
- Collect feedback from end-users regularly
- Combine quantitative metrics with qualitative insights
- Establish baseline metrics before AI implementation

---

## Final Note

A strong KPI framework is essential for successful AI adoption.  
It ensures that AI initiatives are not only implemented, but also **measurably improve business performance, efficiency, and user experience**.
